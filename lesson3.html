The PASO Framework

Modern selling isn't about pushing productsâ€”it's about guiding customers to solutions that genuinely improve their lives. The PASO framework provides a structured yet natural approach to every customer interaction.

P

Probe

Ask strategic questions to uncover both stated and hidden needs. Listen more than you speak, and show genuine curiosity about their situation.

A

Align

Connect specific product features to their expressed needs. Create clear links between what they want and what you're offering.

S

Solve

Address concerns and objections as opportunities to provide valuable information rather than obstacles to overcome.

O

Optimize

Suggest complementary items that enhance their primary purchase, focusing on improved experience rather than just increased sales.

Pro Tip: The best selling technique is the one the customer doesn't recognize as a technique. When done right, effective selling feels like helpful guidance from a knowledgeable friend.

3

Feature-Benefit-Advantage Selling

Transform technical specifications into compelling value propositions by following this sequence:

Smart Watch Example

Feature (What it has)

"This smartwatch has built-in GPS tracking."

Benefit (How it helps)

"So you can track your runs without carrying your phone."

Advantage (Why it's superior)

"This gives you more freedom than fitness trackers that require phone connectivity, and you'll never miss tracking a workout because you forgot your phone."

Remember: Always present in F-B-A order. Leading with technical specifications alone loses most customers' attention within seconds.

Objection Handling Mastery

Customer objections aren't roadblocksâ€”they're opportunities to provide clarity and build trust. Master the LSCR method:

L

Listen

Allow complete expression without interrupting

S

Support

Validate their right to be concerned

C

Counter

Address with relevant information

R

Reaffirm

Circle back to key benefits

Real Example: "It's too expensive"

Listen: [Allow them to fully express their concern]

Support: "I completely understand that budget is important, and it's smart to be careful with investments."

Counter: "What many customers find is that this model's 3-year warranty and energy efficiency actually save money long-term. Plus, we offer 12-month interest-free payments."

Reaffirm: "And since processing speed was your top priority, this will save you hours each week compared to lower-priced alternatives."

5

Closing with Confidence

Different customers respond to different closing approaches. Match your technique to their personality type:

Customer Type
Best Closing Technique
Example Language
The Researcher
Summary Close: Recap how product meets all requirements
"Based on your need for durability, portability, and battery life, this checks all boxes. Anything else to consider?"
The Impulsive
Urgency Close: Emphasize limited availability
"This is our last one in this color. Should I get this ready for you?"
The Wanderer
Experience Close: Focus on positive feelings
"Imagine how great this will look in your living room. Friday or Saturday delivery?"
Budget-Conscious
Value Justification: Emphasize long-term savings
"This 5-year lifespan vs. 2 years for cheaper options saves you $200 long-term. Full payment or financing?"
Watch for Buying Signals: Questions about delivery, warranty details, or payment options indicate readiness to purchase. When you see these signals, move confidently toward closing.

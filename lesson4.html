5Color, Lighting, and Sensory Secrets

  • Color Psychology: Blue = trust; red = urgency; green = freshness; yellow = optimism. Use color to drive feelings and focus.
  • Lighting: Spotlight new or premium products. Warm lighting = inviting; cool lighting = modern/fresh. Use accent lighting on displays and mannequins.
  • Scents & Sounds: Scented zones and curated playlists can increase time in-store by 18%.
üåà Fun Fact: Stores with music matching their brand (tempo, genre) see a 9% higher average spend!

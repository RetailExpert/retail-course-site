2The 8 Primary Retail Personality Types

  • The Mission Shopper: On a mission, wants efficiency. Approach: Be direct, offer fast solutions.
  • The Browser: No clear goal, open to discovery. Approach: Spark curiosity, offer suggestions.
  • The Value Seeker: Motivated by deals. Approach: Highlight promotions, loyalty programs.
  • The Quality Connoisseur: Seeks best-in-class. Approach: Focus on craftsmanship, warranties.
  • The Social Shopper: Buys for others, wants validation. Approach: Use social proof, stories.
  • The Researcher: Already informed, detail-oriented. Approach: Provide expert info, specs.
  • The Impulse Buyer: Responds to emotion, urgency. Approach: Use scarcity, FOMO language.
  • The Loyalist: Repeat customer, expects recognition. Approach: Personalize, give VIP perks.

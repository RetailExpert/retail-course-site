💰 The Salon Goldmine

The real money is in WAITING TIME and RETAIL PRODUCTS.

A client waits 30-90 minutes. That's your opportunity to make an extra R200-R500 per client WITHOUT doing more hair. Most salons ignore this — that's R8,000+ lost every month.

The 3 Money Multipliers

1. The Waiting Room Shop: Display products at eye level while clients wait. Easy R200-R300 per client from boredom + trust.

2. The Deposit Psychology: R50-R100 deposit cuts no-shows by 65%. Your time = money. Stop working for free.

3. The Next Appointment Rule: Book the next appointment BEFORE they leave. 80% will keep it. Guaranteed income stream.

Position your retail display so clients see it in the mirror while getting their hair done.

They're already in "beauty investment" mode. Seeing products in the mirror = 40% higher chance of buying. This one change adds R5,000+ monthly.

The 3 Money Multipliers

1. The Atmosphere ROI: Good music + clean space = people stay 2 hours longer = 3 more rounds per person = R600 extra per table. R500/month investment = R12,000+ back.

2. The Regular System: Know 30 regulars by name + drink preference. They bring friends. "This is MY spot" loyalty = exponential growth.

Say: "Last round in 15 minutes, gents!" — never "last round NOW".

People order 2 rounds — one now, one at the 15-minute mark. That's an extra R2,000+ per night you would have missed.
